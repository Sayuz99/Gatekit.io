GateKit — embed demo

Primary trigger
Use on your contact button or support link.
Ghost variant
For secondary placement in nav or footer.
Accent variant
High-visibility CTA, e.g. inside a product.
JS API
Open the gate from your own code.
Embed snippet
<!-- Add before </body> — replace "yourname" with your Gate ID -->
<script
  src="https://cdn.gatekit.io/gate.js"
  data-id="yourname"
  data-price="1"
  data-brand="true"
></script>

<!-- Then trigger it anywhere: -->
<button data-gk-trigger>Contact support →</button>

<!-- Or via JS: -->
<script>GateKit.open()</script>
